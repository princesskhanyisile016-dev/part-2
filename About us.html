About Us

Welcome to Youth Assistant! We are a team of dedicated professionals committed to supporting young people as they navigate their educational and career paths.

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality resources and guidance to help you achieve your goals and build a successful future.

Founded in 2018, Youth Assistant has grown into a trusted partner for students across South Africa, offering personalized advice, practical tools, and a supportive community.

Whether you’re looking for help with university applications, study tips, or digital skills, we are here to support you every step of the way.

Our team is passionate about empowering young people to make informed decisions and take confident steps toward their dreams. We believe that with the right guidance and resources, every student can succeed.

Thank you for being part of the Youth Assistant community. We look forward to helping you achieve your goals and build a bright future!

From course guidance to accommodation advice and digital skills training, we are here to provide the support you need to succeed.

About Youth Assistant

Our Story

Youth Assistant began as a small support group for students who needed help with course choices, university applications, and access to digital tools. Today, we serve learners across South Africa with practical guidance, personal encouragement, and easy-to-follow resources.

Who We Help

We support matriculants, first-year students, parents, and community members who want to learn how to use technology, apply for university, or find safe student accommodation.

What Makes Us Different

We combine local knowledge with friendly support: helping you understand your options, giving clear next steps, and offering real-world tips that make study and student life simpler.

Our Approach

At Youth Assistant, we listen first. Then we create a plan that fits your goals, budget, and timeline. Whether you need help choosing a course, applying for a scholarship, or improving your computer skills, our guidance is practical and supportive.

Get Involved

If you want guidance, have a question, or want to share your story, use the Contact page to reach out. We’re here to help you move forward with confidence.
