About Us

Our History

Youth Assistant was established in 2018 to assist students with educational guidance and digital support. Since then, we’ve supported learners across South Africa in taking their next steps with confidence.

Mission

Our mission is to provide clear, caring, and practical support to students, matriculants, and anyone needing help with education and technology.

To provide clear, caring, and practical support to students, matriculants, and anyone needing help with education and technology.

We believe that with the right guidance and resources, every young person can feel prepared to take the next step toward their education and housing goals.

We are committed to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment where every student feels valued and supported. We believe in the power of education to transform lives and are dedicated to helping you access the opportunities you deserve.

Vision

To grow into a trusted partner for learners and families throughout South Africa, helping them access the right information, resources, and support.

Values

We are guided by respect, honesty, inclusion, and a commitment to helping every young person feel prepared and supported.

We understand that the journey to higher education and career success can be challenging, which is why we offer a wide range of resources and personalized support to help you overcome obstacles and achieve your goals. From course guidance to accommodation advice and digital skills training, we are here to provide the support you need to succeed.

Our team is made up of experienced educators, career advisors, and digital experts who are passionate about helping students succeed. We understand the challenges you face and are here to provide the support you need to overcome them.

Community Impact

We work with local schools and community groups to share study advice, application guidance, and digital literacy skills, so students can succeed on campus and beyond.

We are proud of the impact we have made in the lives of students across South Africa and are committed to continuing to grow and evolve to meet your needs. Thank you for being part of our community, and we look forward to supporting you on your journey to success!
